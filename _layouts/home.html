Impeccable Service

Sharp Cleaners has been offering expert dry cleaning service since 1999. At Sharp Cleaners your garments are handled with care. We offer dry cleaning, tailoring, alterations, wash & fold laundry service, shoe repairs, as well as two full service laundromat locations.

Environmentally Friendly

Sharp Cleaners is committed to being green. We use only non-hazardous and non-toxic cleaning methods to keep your clothing looking and feeling like new.

Expert Tailoring

Our experienced seamstress and tailor are available for fittings and offer expert service. Trust Sharp Cleaners for all of your alteration needs including button and zipper replacement, hemming, waist alterations, leather & suede repairs, as well as bridal gown and prom dress alterations.
